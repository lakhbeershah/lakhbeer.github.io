About

More About Me.

I am a dedicated AWS Cloud Engineer based in Delhi, India. I have over 2+ years of experience at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where I specialize in on-premise and cloud-based data environments.

My focus is on Amazon Redshift, ETL processes, and data automation. I am proficient in SQL, AWS services such as CodePipeline and Athena, and data visualization using Tableau. I am known for my ability to streamline data migration, optimize queries, and implement AWS automation solutions to improve productivity and data reliability.

I hold a Bachelor of Technology degree in Computer Science from PDM University and I am an AWS Solutions Architect - Associate. I have worked on several projects, including the integration of Amazon Redshift and RDS PostgreSQL with DBeaver, and automated data migration from on-premise databases to Amazon S3. These projects have showcased my ability to develop efficient data management solutions, secure data access, and streamline database operations. I continue to leverage my skills and knowledge to drive data efficiency and reliability in the cloud environment. Thank you
